mountain

Mountain forests, or montane forests, are ecosystems found at high elevations, typically above 1,000 meters, characterized by cool temperatures, high precipitation, and diverse plant and animal life adapted to the harsh conditions of mountainous terrain.

green forest

A "green forest" refers to a forest characterized by abundant, green-leaved trees, often associated with a sense of tranquility and biodiversity. Here's a more detailed look at what makes a forest .A green forest is simply a forest where the dominant vegetation, the trees, have green leaves.
